Board of Trustees

Paul Mamalian

President

Paul Mamalian serves as President of Journey for Good Foundation, Inc. He provides leadership for the organization's mission, strategic planning, and community engagement efforts. He works closely with trustees, volunteers, and community partners to advance programs that create meaningful and lasting impact.

Chairman of the Board

Steve Johnson serves as Chairman of the Board, helping guide the foundation's governance, long-term vision, and organizational oversight. He works with fellow trustees to support responsible stewardship and ensure the foundation remains focused on its charitable mission.

Jake Crandlemire

Secretary & Treasurer

Jake (Jason) Crandlemire serves as Secretary and Treasurer of the foundation. He supports board administration, organizational records, financial stewardship, and accountability while helping ensure the foundation operates with transparency and integrity.
